Research

I previously focused on Open-World learning, particularly in the area of novel class discovery, with the goal of enabling models to cluster new classes autonomously. Currently, I am interested in Optimal Transport, foundational models and 3D\4D understanding.

I am seeking a Post-doc position in Fall 2025.
