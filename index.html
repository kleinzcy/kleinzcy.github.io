Research

I previously focused on Open-World learning, particularly in the area of novel class discovery, with the goal of enabling models to identify new classes autonomously. Currently, my interests have shifted towards evaluating, understanding, and enhancing the reasoning capabilities of large language models (LLMs) and vision-language models (VLMs).

I am seeking a Post-doc position or Job in Large language models or Vision language models in Fall 2025.
